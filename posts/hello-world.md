Welcome to the Freeborn Charles blog — my little corner of the internet where I write about the modern web.

I'm Charles Freeborn, a front-end developer and technical writer. I spend most of my time working with JavaScript, React, and the broader JAMstack ecosystem — and I've decided it's time to start sharing what I learn publicly.

## What to Expect

This blog will be a space for me to:

- Write about JavaScript and its ecosystem — things I'm learning, patterns I find useful, and problems I've solved
- Explore React, Gatsby, and the JAMstack in depth
- Share notes, opinions, and ideas on modern web development
- Document my journey as a developer and technical writer

## Why Start a Blog?

Writing is thinking. The act of trying to explain something forces you to actually understand it. I've consumed a lot of content over the years, and I think it's time to start contributing back.

I also believe in learning in public. By sharing what I'm working through — even the messy, unfinished parts — I hope to connect with others on the same path.

## Let's Connect

If you find anything useful here, or want to talk web development, find me on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/charliecodes) or [GitHub](https://github.com/charlesfreeborn).

Here's to the journey.
